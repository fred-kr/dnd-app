Item Notes
Torches Burn for 1 hour.
Oil flasks Lanterns burn for 6 hours per oil flask.
Rations 1 lb. of food per day.
Water 1 gallon per day (2 gallons if hot).
Ammunition 5 daggers, 20 arrows, bolts, darts, or bullets, or 50 needles fill 1 slot.

Encumbrance

Status Effect
Unencumbered Normal speed.
Encumbered Your speed drops by 10 feet.
Heavily encumbered Your speed drops by 20 feet and you have disadvantage on ability checks, attack rolls, and saving throws that use Strength, Dexterity, or Constitution.

You can carry items that fill a number of slots equal to your Strength score without penalty. If you carry more than your Strength score, you are encumbered. You cannot carry more than three times your Strength score.

These limits are doubled for Large creatures and halved for Tiny creatures. Dwarves may ignore penalties to speed.
